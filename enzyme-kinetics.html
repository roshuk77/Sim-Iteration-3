Overview

This module explores enzyme kinetics by simulating reaction rates based on classical biochemical models.

Key Equations

  • Michaelis-Menten Equation: v = (Vmax[S]) / (Km + [S])
  • Lineweaver-Burk Equation: 1/v = (Km / Vmax) * (1 / [S]) + 1 / Vmax
  • General Reaction Rate: v = kcat[E][S] / (Km + [S])
