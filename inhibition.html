General Kinetics:

$$v = \dfrac{V_{max}[S]}{K_m + [S]}$$

Competitive Inhibition:

$$v = \dfrac{V_{max}[S]}{K_m\left(1 + \dfrac{[I]}{K_i}\right) + [S]}$$
